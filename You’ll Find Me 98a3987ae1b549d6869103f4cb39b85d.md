# You’ll Find Me

Article Privacy: Published
Life Chapter: Love Life
Life Period: 21-25
Tags: Poetry
Entry: 05/03/2017

Bare feet on soft earth

Sunshine on warm skin

And that's where you'll find me

Also probably near the sea
