# Beauty of Art

Yet, in that beauty of art which we need to survive,
it is made with the most broken hearts
that perhaps wish to die.

Isn’t that a curious paradox

Our survival for a broken heart
depends on the art we create
from its brokenness.

– Lisa T., Art
